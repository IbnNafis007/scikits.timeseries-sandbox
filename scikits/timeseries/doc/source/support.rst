Support
=======

No commercial support is offered for the scikits.timeseries module. Requests
for help should be directed to the
`scipy-user mailing list
<http://projects.scipy.org/mailman/listinfo/scipy-user>`_ .

Bug reports should be filed in the
`scikits bug tracker
<http://scipy.org/scipy/scikits/query?component=timeseries&order=status>`_ .
